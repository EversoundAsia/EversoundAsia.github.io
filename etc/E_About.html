About Us

Eversound Asia was established in 2015 in collaboration with John Adorney, an outstanding Los Angeles-based composer. After hearing John's heartfelt and evocative music, we were inspired to expand the influence of this music, and that of other artists with similar talents, to the Asian market

Company's Vision

Producing and marketing of quality music that would resonate with listeners globally.


We hope to bridge the music cultures of the East and West to initiate a new era of music.

Values and Propositions

Our key focus at EverSound Asia is to work with artists and corporations on a very personal level to create music that fully reflects their branding.
